How it works

Enter the patient's 13 clinical features below. The system runs a soft-voting ensemble of Random Forest, SVM, and ANN, then generates SHAP and LIME explanations alongside a downloadable clinical PDF report.

Demographics

Age
Patient age in years
Sex
1 = Male, 0 = Female

Cardiac Symptoms

Chest Pain Type (cp)
0=Typical angina, 1=Atypical, 2=Non-anginal, 3=Asymptomatic
Exercise Induced Angina
1 = Yes, 0 = No
ST Depression (oldpeak)
ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest
Slope of ST Segment
0=Upsloping, 1=Flat, 2=Downsloping

Vitals & Lab Results

Resting Blood Pressure
mmHg on admission
Cholesterol (chol)
Serum cholesterol in mg/dl
Fasting Blood Sugar
1 if fasting BS > 120 mg/dl
Max Heart Rate (thalach)
Maximum heart rate achieved

Diagnostic Tests

Resting ECG (restecg)
0=Normal, 1=ST-T abnormality, 2=LV hypertrophy
Coronary Arteries (ca)
Number of major vessels coloured by fluoroscopy
Thalassemia (thal)
0=Normal, 1=Fixed defect, 2=Reversible defect, 3=Other
